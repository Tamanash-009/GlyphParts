# GlyphParts v1.0

Independent archive for Nothing and CMF spare-part prices.

## Highlights
- **PWA Support:** Installable app with offline capabilities.
- **Offline Mode:** Local persistence ensures immediate load times.
- **Device Database:** Accurate component tracking across Flagship, A Series, and CMF.
- **Search System:** Instant, keyboard-accessible (⌘K) unified search.
- **Service Center Discovery:** Find the nearest official center without commercial tracking.
- **Theming:** Full Dark and Light mode support.
- **Community Sourced:** Built-in "Report Incorrect Price" pipelines.
- **Legal Compliance:** Transparent disclaimer, privacy, and terms pages.
- **Changelog & SEO:** Integrated release history and JSON-LD structured data for discovery.
